Interns

Gary Clarke

Palo Alto, CA
Customer Support

Gary is very excited about joining CK-12. After spending many years in educational publishing and IT Web publishing, he wanted to work for a top-notch team that brought those two worlds together. Delivering high quality online educational content over the Web and to build a thriving online educational community, all in support of an open education mission...now what could be better than that! For the past nine-plus years, Gary has gained experience in online Web publishing and online community building with Catapulse, Rational Software,  a little company called IBM (developerWorks) and an even smaller one, Microsoft (MSDN). Prior to that, he spent six years with Pearson and Key Curriculum Press. He attended the London School of Economics, University of London, University of Southern California, and taught logic and philosophy for one year at Manchester University.  He has a wife, Jozefa, and two very small children, Oliver (4 years) and Abigail (14 months.)  He has no free time whatsoever!
